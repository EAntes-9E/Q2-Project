Mango Float

Ingredients

  • Ripe mangoes: Sliced thinly
  • Graham crackers: Crushed or whole
  • All-purpose cream: Whipped
  • Condensed milk: Sweetened
  • Milk: Evaporated or fresh (optional, to adjust consistency)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the cream mixture: In a bowl, combine all-purpose cream and condensed milk. Whip together until smooth and creamy.
  2. Layer the dessert: In a dish, arrange a layer of graham crackers at the bottom. Spread a layer of the cream mixture over the crackers. Then, add a layer of sliced mangoes on top.
  3. Repeat: Repeat the layering process (graham crackers, cream, mangoes) until all ingredients are used, ending with a cream layer on top.
  4. Chill: Cover and refrigerate the dessert for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld and the dessert to set.
  5. Serve: Serve chilled, topped with extra mango slices for garnish.
