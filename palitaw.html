Palitaw

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Water
  • Sugar (for coating)
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Grated coconut (optional for coating)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough: In a bowl, mix the glutinous rice flour, a pinch of salt, and water to form a dough. The dough should be smooth and sticky.
  2. Shape the dough: Roll the dough into small balls and flatten each ball into a disc shape using your hands.
  3. Cook the palitaw: Boil water in a large pot. Once itâ€™s boiling, drop the discs of dough into the water. When they float to the surface, they are cooked. Let them float for a few seconds before removing them with a slotted spoon.
  4. Coat with sugar and sesame seeds: Immediately coat the palitaw in a mixture of sugar, toasted sesame seeds, and optionally grated coconut for added flavor and texture.
  5. Serve: Arrange them on a plate and serve warm.
