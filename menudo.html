Menudo

Ingredients

  • Pork: Pork shoulder or belly, cut into small cubes)
  • Pork liver: Typically pig's liver, diced
  • Potatoes: Peeled and diced
  • 1 radish (labanos), peeled and sliced thinly
  • Carrots: Diced
  • Bell peppers: Red and green, diced
  • Garlic: Minced
  • Onion: Chopped
  • Tomatoes: Chopped or canned tomato sauce/paste
  • Raisins: Adds sweetness to balance the flavors (optional)
  • Garbanzo beans (chickpeas): Optional, for added texture
  • Bay leaves: For flavor
  • Peas: Optional
  • Chicken or beef broth: For a savory base
  • Soy sauce: For marinating the meat
  • Fish sauce: For umami flavor
  • Salt and pepper: To taste
  • Tomato sauce: For the base of the sauce
  • Sugar: Optional, to balance acidity

Instructions

  1. Marinate pork: Marinate the pork in soy sauce, garlic, and pepper for about 15-30 minutes.
  2. Sauté aromatics: In a large pan, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant. Add the tomatoes and cook until softened.
  3. Cook the pork: Add the marinated pork and cook until browned.
  4. Simmer: Pour in the tomato sauce, chicken broth, and bay leaves. Let it simmer until the pork is tender.
  5. Add liver and vegetables: Stir in the pork liver, potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, and any other vegetables. Add raisins if desired. Continue to cook until everything is tender and well-combined.
  6. Season: Add fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Adjust the sweetness or seasoning as needed.
