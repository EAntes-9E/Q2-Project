Kare-Kare

Ingredients

  • 1 kg oxtail, cut into serving pieces (can substitute with beef shank, pork hocks, or tripe)
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Salt
  • 1 banana heart
  • 1 bunch of string beans (sitaw)
  • 1 sliced eggplant
  • 1 bunch of bok choy (pechay)
  • 1/2 cup annatto seeds/powder
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of toasted rice flour
  • 2 cups of broth
  • 1/4 cup of bagoong alamang

Instructions

  1. Boil the oxtail (or meat of choice) with onion, garlic, and salt until tender. Set the broth aside.
  2. Dissolve annatto seeds in warm water (or use annatto powder) for color.
  3. Mix peanut butter (or ground roasted peanuts) with the broth. Add toasted rice flour as a thickener.
  4. Blanch the banana heart, string beans, eggplant, and bok choy in boiling water until tender.
  5. In a pot, mix the cooked meat with the peanut sauce. Simmer until the sauce thickens and is well combined.
  6. Arrange the vegetables on top or alongside the stew.
  7. Serve hot with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) on the side. Enjoy!
