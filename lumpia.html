Lumpia

Ingredients

  • 500g of ground pork
  • 1 small grated carrot
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped green onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 30-40 pieces of lumpia wrappers
  • Water or beaten egg
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix ground pork, grated carrots, chopped onions, garlic, egg, soy sauce, green onions, salt, pepper, and any additional seasonings until well combined.
  2. Lay a lumpia wrapper flat. Place 1–2 tablespoons of the filling near one edge.
  3. Roll tightly, folding in the sides as you go. Seal the edge with water or beaten egg. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the lumpia in batches until golden brown and crisp, about 3–5 minutes per batch.
  5. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot with sweet chili sauce, banana ketchup, or your preferred dipping sauce.
